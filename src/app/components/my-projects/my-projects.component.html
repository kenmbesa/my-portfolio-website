Completed Projects

In 2021, a few weeks after deciding to learn how to code, I realized that the best and quickest way to understand the principles of programming was to work on real-world projects, rather than watching tutorials and copying the code provided by the tutors. So I started working on simple frontend projects like signup forms, info boxes, page sections and single page wesite UIs. With time, I realized I had gradually built a library of completed projects that I'm today proud to showcase to friends, strangers and potential clients.
